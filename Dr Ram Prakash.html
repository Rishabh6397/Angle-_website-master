Personal Biography

Dr Mira Thapa is a Senior Gynecologist and Specialist in Advanced Infertility and Endoscopic Surgeries. A medical graduate of College of Medical Science (CMSTH), Dr Thapa went on to complete her post graduation in Gyne/Obs from TUTH, followed by a Sub- specialty Fellowship in Advanced Infertility and Endoscopic Surgeries at the Grant Medical Foundation Ruby Hall Clinic IVF and Endoscopy Centre in Pune, India .Dr. Thapa has obtained further academic qualifications including a Fellowship in Cosmetic gynecology by the American Board of Cosmetic Gynecology (ABCG) from Dubai in 2021. She is currently fulfilling her duties as Director of Angel Fertility Clinic.

Her career as a doctor started from being Medical Officer at College of Medical Science, Chitawan from the year 2004. She then served a Senior Medical Officer at Himal Hospital in kathmandu followed by being a registrar at civil Service Hospital year 2012. She is working as as Consultant Gynecologist at Sumeru Hospital ever since. Dr. Thapa is also associated with Vayodha Hospital, and Sumeru City Hospital since 2012.

Dr. Thapa co-founded NIFLC Maya IVF in the year 2013 and Angel Fertility Clinic in the year 2020 to provide patients with quality infertility treatment. She uses her contemporary, evidence based knowledge, clinical experience and common sense to allow his patients to explore options. She sees this as essential to creating a care plan that they understand, are happy with and addresses their unique set of needs. Dr. Mira Thapa loves helping patients suffering from all types of infertility. She particularly identifies with and is passionate about reproductive options for heterosexual couples, as well as increasing awareness and access to social egg freezing.
